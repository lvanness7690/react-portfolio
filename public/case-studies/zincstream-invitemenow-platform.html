Zincstream and InviteMeNow Platform

Streaming, registration, and client-facing event experience systems

Overview

Zincstream began as a proprietary streaming and registration platform built during Zinc Agency's COVID pivot. When in-person events returned, I repurposed that same infrastructure into InviteMeNow, a branded event-registration platform for in-person, virtual, and hybrid hospitality programs.

Problem

The business needed a way to preserve premium client programming when in-person events shut down, then later needed a reusable registration and event-information platform that did not depend entirely on expensive external event SaaS.

What I Built

  • Custom streaming and registration workflows using WordPress, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and embedded meeting and video APIs including Whereby
  • Branded client event pages, custom URLs and subdomains, and reusable registration flows
  • Support for more than 2,000 celebrity-led livestreams during the virtual programming pivot
  • InviteMeNow event pages with speaker and venue information, maps, galleries, register and thank-you states, and support contact flows
  • A practical alternative to event SaaS subscriptions such as Splash for client-facing hospitality programs

Technical Stack

WordPress, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Whereby and embedded meeting/video APIs, custom registration workflows, branded content systems, and client-facing event operations.

Impact

This platform showed that I could build a business-critical system under changing conditions, preserve client delivery through a major operating shift, and then evolve the same infrastructure into a more durable product for in-person, virtual, and hybrid experiences.

Hive Rollout & Workflow Automation

Business systems, workflow automation, and cross-functional operating structure

Overview

At Zinc Agency, I identified Hive as the right project-management platform for the division, led the rollout, and built the custom workflows and automations that turned it into a real operating system for the team. The system was strong enough that it carried forward into Elevate Experiences and is still used daily.

Business Context

The division needed a better way to stay organized across complex client programs, internal handoffs, production work, logistics, and account activity. Work was spread across too many disconnected processes, which made it harder to maintain consistency, scale delivery, and keep different teams aligned.

This was not just a tool-selection problem. The challenge was to introduce a system people would actually adopt, then shape it around the real workflow of the business.

Problem

The team needed a shared operating system for project and task management, better visibility into work status and ownership, stronger connections between project operations and systems like Salesforce, and fewer manual handoffs.

What I Built

  • Identified Hive as the right platform and led the implementation for the division
  • Designed custom workflows that matched how the business actually operated instead of forcing the team into a generic task structure
  • Built out workflow rules, intake structure, forms, and operating conventions so the platform could support repeatable execution
  • Connected Hive to Salesforce and related operational tools through custom automations and backend workflow logic
  • Helped automate recurring actions, status movement, and coordination steps that would otherwise require manual follow-up
  • Supported adoption across users so the platform became part of the daily operating rhythm rather than a side tool

Systems and Integrations

Hive, Salesforce, Zapier and workflow automations, custom forms and intake structure, and division-wide project and task management processes.

Impact

The rollout improved visibility, reduced manual coordination, and gave the division a more reliable shared system for organizing work. More importantly, it created operational consistency that supported growth: the team could stay organized, move faster, and manage more complexity without depending on scattered one-off processes.

The strongest proof point is durability. This was not a short-lived implementation. The system carried forward from Zinc Agency into Elevate Experiences and remains part of how the business runs.

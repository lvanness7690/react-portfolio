Inventory & Logistics Tracker

Google Forms, Google Sheets, Apps Script, and event operations automation

Overview

I built and automated an inventory and logistics tracker for a recurring three-year event series spanning roughly 60 events, 600 shipped bins, and about 12,000 to 24,000 tracked items.

Problem

The event team needed a reliable way to manage event intake, shipment status, destination routing, low inventory, damaged-item replacement, recap emails, and operational recovery flows across a large recurring program.

What I Built

  • Google Forms intake workflow
  • Google Sheets data model for intake, schedule, dashboard, and replacement logs
  • Apps Script automation for recap emails, low-inventory alerts, and damaged-item notifications
  • Submission-status syncing and replacement-record appending
  • Safe resend and delete-cascade recovery flows

Impact

The tracker reduced manual operations, improved reporting consistency, and gave the team a cleaner way to manage inventory and logistics across a high-volume recurring event program.

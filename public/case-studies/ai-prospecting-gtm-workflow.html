AI Prospecting & GTM Workflow

AI workflow adoption, research automation, and GTM systems

Overview

I built and operationalized AI-assisted prospecting workflows using Claude, OpenAI, ZoomInfo, Outreach, structured prompts, and human review.

Problem

Prospecting and outbound preparation were manual, inconsistent, and time-consuming. The team needed repeatable ways to research companies, enrich contacts, organize target lists, and support campaigns.

Workflow

  • Define campaign goals and target criteria
  • Use Claude and OpenAI for structured company and account research
  • Use ZoomInfo to enrich and validate company and contact data
  • Prepare Outreach-ready prospect lists and campaign inputs
  • Review outputs manually before use

Impact

The workflow helped the team move from broad target ideas to actionable prospect lists more quickly while establishing a practical model for AI adoption inside daily business operations.

Elevate Website Launch & GTM Systems

Website launch coordination, GTM systems support, and post-launch operations

Overview

At Elevate Experiences, I helped lead the launch of the new marketing site and then took on the ongoing operational work required to keep it effective. That meant coordinating developers, design, copy, stakeholder review, QA, and launch readiness, then managing the site as an active growth and client-engagement system tied to content, SEO, analytics, forms, and CRM workflows.

Business Context

Elevate sells premium corporate hospitality and experiential programming across categories like culinary, wine and spirits, entertainment, sports, fashion/art, and thought leadership. The website needed to communicate that breadth clearly, support high-end brand positioning, and help turn interest into qualified conversations.

This was not just a brochure site. It needed to function as a live GTM asset for a company selling complex, high-touch experiences.

Problem

The business needed a site that could present multiple experience categories clearly, support premium brand positioning, showcase success stories and testimonials, capture inbound interest effectively, connect website activity to CRM and marketing workflows, and stay current as offerings and campaign priorities evolved.

What I Did

  • Helped coordinate the website launch across development, design, copywriting, stakeholder review, QA, launch readiness, and follow-up improvements
  • Supported a modular site structure built around experience categories, capabilities, featured experiences, success stories, testimonials, and inquiry flows
  • Managed post-launch website operations, including weekly content updates, refinements, and quality control
  • Maintained SEO hygiene and monitored analytics to support discoverability and site performance
  • Supported form, campaign, and CRM workflows tied to lead capture and demand generation
  • Helped connect website content, inbound inquiry handling, marketing operations, and sales follow-through into a more coherent system

What The Site Shows

The current preview includes category landing pages, featured experiences, success stories, testimonial sections, service and capabilities framing for corporate buyers, and inquiry flows designed to capture inbound interest. At the system level, that means the site supports both brand positioning and operational follow-through, not just visual presentation.

Systems and Integrations

Website launch coordination, QA and stakeholder review workflows, SEO hygiene and Google Analytics monitoring, HubSpot form workflows, Salesforce campaign tracking and CRM support, and content operations aligned with GTM needs.

Impact

The site gave Elevate a stronger public-facing platform for presenting its services, proof points, and premium positioning. Operationally, it also created a better bridge between marketing, inbound interest, and sales follow-through through cleaner content operations and CRM-connected lead flows.
